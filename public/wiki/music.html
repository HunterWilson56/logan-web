Commands

Ah. Music. It sound nice. And it was a bit tricky to code. Keep in mind. To use music you must have a role called Pleogg DJ spelled Exactly like that.

Play

Usage: l.play [SongTitle / YoutubeURL]
Description: Play a song. Make sure you are in a voice channel to use it.

Favorite

Usage: l.fa[Number That I forgot ]
Description: Play a song. Make sure you are in a voice channel to use it. Put in a number and it will play a song from the favorites queue. Just don't use it. Its too complex.

Stop

Usage: l.stop
Description: Stops the music playing right now.

Skip

Usage: l.skip
Description: Skips the current song.

Queue

Usage: l.queue
Description: Check whats on the queue.

Volume

Usage: l.vol [1/5]
Description: Set the volume lower or higher.
